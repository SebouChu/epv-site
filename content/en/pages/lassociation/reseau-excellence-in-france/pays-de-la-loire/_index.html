meta_description: > description: > summary: > Founded in 2018, the Association Régionale des EPV en Pays de la Loire promotes the region's emblematic companies that excel in innovation and know-how, preserving heritage and anticipating the future, as well as in their local and international influence. header_text: >- header_cta: display: false label: target: contents_reading_time: seconds: 54 text: >- 1 minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- MISSIONS & ACTIONS ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

The association is at the service of all the EPV companies in the Pays de la Loire region, providing a network for exchanges between professionals, as well as organising regular events to highlight the expertise of the region's EPV-labelled companies.

We act as a communications link between them and the various national and regional players. We also help companies to create their application or renewal dossier for the label. Finally, we have developed a programme to safeguard the know-how for our members, in order to preserve what makes them special.

The association's objectives are to:  

  • Bring together all the EPVs in the Pays de la Loire region
  • Create a network of exchanges and new synergies
  • Organise concrete actions (participation in trade fairs in the region and beyond, joint website, etc.)
  • Develop our presence on social networks as a showcase for approved companies
  • Monitor changes in labelling criteria

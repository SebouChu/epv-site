Combining spinning and deep drawing for over 30 yearsWe are one of the few French players to combine spinning and deep-drawing, thanks to a team of loyal, highly-experienced journeymen with an average length of service of over 10 years. This complementarity enables us to advise you on the best process according to "shape/material/quantity", in order to control the cost of your parts.

  • 3 hydraulic presses,
  • 2 mechanical presses,
  • 2 digital spinning lathes.

We work with all sheet metals, and specialize in stainless steel.

Terre Figuière, created in 1839, is a French company renowned for its Anduze potteries, artisanal pieces of enameled terracotta. The artisans of Terre Figuière master with virtuosity the shaping and glazing techniques inherited from the master potters of yesteryear. Each step of the process, from modeling to firing to glazing, is carried out with care and precision, ensuring the quality and durability of each piece.

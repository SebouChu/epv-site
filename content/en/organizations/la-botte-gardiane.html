Created in 1958 and today managed by the second Agulhon generation, La Botte Gardiane produces, with unique know-how, shoes and leather articles in Camargue, in Gard. Sold on 5 continents, the brand offers more than 220 models of boots, ankle boots, sandals, shoes... which are offered with a customization and tailor-made service. Production is carried out in a short circuit and does not use any subcontractors. La Botte Gardianne ensures the greatest transparency on the origin of its products.

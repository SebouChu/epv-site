A specialist and national leader in the manufacture of plain and Jacquard velvets for clothing and furnishings, we own three brands: Bouton Renaud, Soies de France and Velours Blafo.

With a rich history spanning more than 3 generations, our company reconciles tradition and innovation, know-how and creation, work and passion, heritage and future, regional and international, all while perpetuating perfect quality.

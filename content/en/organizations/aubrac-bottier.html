Aubrac Bottier manufactures in France and by hand very high quality shoes, in the traditions and know-how inherited from the most renowned houses. All leather hides and soles are of 1st choice and come from Aveyron tanneries; The company also uses VIBRAM rubber soles and a sewn assembly to guarantee flexibility and robustness for daily use. Aubrac Bottier has chosen to work in a short circuit in an eco-responsible approach, with the trust of the best suppliers and distributors and to guarantee French manufacturing.

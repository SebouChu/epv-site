SAS Jean Marie Bot, an emblematic jeweler in Narbonne, embodies five generations of know-how and passion for the creation of refined jewellery. Their renowned craftsmanship offers jewellery of remarkable finesse, ranging from custom-made creations to pieces in gold, silver and precious stones. Their shop also offers repair and restoration services, in a warm and personalized atmosphere.

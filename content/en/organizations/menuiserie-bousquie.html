Master craftsman in crafts, carpenter and cabinetmaker, with 33 years of experience, Christophe Bousquié completed his training with the companions of duty. He specializes in the restoration of old building heritage and historical monuments. The company works with local woods such as oak, chestnut, ash, pine, larch, etc. to create or restore entrance doors and carriage doors, windows with interior shutters, staircases, assembled parquet floors called "versailles", woodwork, restoration of old carpentry, turning and sculpture. It also manufactures rustic and contemporary kitchens, interior designs and furniture. The workshop is located north of the Parc des Cévennes in the Valdonnez valley near Mende

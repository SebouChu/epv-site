Briqueterie Capelle, a family business since 1879, perpetuates the ancestral tradition of manufacturing Toulouse brick. Recognized for its artisanal know-how and the quality of its products, Briqueterie Capelle offers baked or raw terracotta bricks, ideal for the construction and renovation of buildings.

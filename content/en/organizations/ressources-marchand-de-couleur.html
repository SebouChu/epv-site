Ressource is a family paint and wallpaper publishing house, heir to artisanal know-how which consisted of exploiting the natural material from the ocher quarries of Roussillon in Provence since the end of the 1940s. Coming from a long tradition of paint manufacturing and a very ancient mastery of color, it is one of the last independent French paint manufacturers.. Over the years and various collaborations with passionate creators, Ressource has enriched its catalog with more than 1000 shades so that all projects can find absolute harmony. Ressource places ecology at the heart of its development and favors sustainable manufacturing.

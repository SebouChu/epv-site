The PALAT Carrières et TP company has been based in northern Aveyron since 1968. It mines and works local stone: schist. They specialize in paving, building stone, roofing slate and mulching. Their know-how is unique, it is becoming rarer: today, there are less than 6 companies in France producing slate.

The Rozière company has been in existence for almost 50 years and offers more than 100 different models of doors, from noble species such as Oak or Beech. It is possible to have doors for new constructions or renovations, hinged or sliding doors, standard or custom-made as well as closet doors. The Rozière production workshops are located in Bozouls in Aveyron (12). Rozière is made up of more than 130 men and women who work every day, with passion and pride, to safeguard know-how.

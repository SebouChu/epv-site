The company ABC Application du Bois Concept specializes in interior and exterior carpentry. Artisan carpenters, the company is committed to providing high quality work. L'Application du Bois is a family business which has been operating since 1967, the date of its creation by Mr Raymond Casane. Since January 2015, Mr. Alran has taken over the company; always surrounded by the same teams and the same companions. Application of wood continues to grow and confirm its technical skills for historical monuments, industrial buildings and in the field of aeronautics.
